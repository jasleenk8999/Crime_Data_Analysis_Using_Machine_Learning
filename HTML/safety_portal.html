Public Safety and Emergency Resources

ALWAYS CALL EMERGENCY SERVICES FIRST FOR LIFE-THREATENING SITUATIONS.

Essential Safety Tips

  • Personal Security: Be aware of your surroundings, especially when using your phone in public.
  • Home Security: Always lock doors and windows, even when leaving for a short time.
  • Cyber Safety: Use strong, unique passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
  • Traveling Alone: Inform a trusted contact of your route and estimated arrival time.
  • Reporting: If you see something suspicious, report it via the Tip Submission Portal.
